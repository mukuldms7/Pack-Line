About
Us

We, MAS SYSTECH PVT LTD, (formerly MAS Enterprises) are a Pune, India based company involved in design, manufacturing and supply of Packaging Machines and related equipment's.

Our Packaging Machines & Solutions serve the need of regional and local companies in Food, Beverages, Liquor, Glass, Edible Oil, Personal Care and Home Care sectors. We take pride in being OEM suppliers to blue chip companies like Tetra Pak, Bosch & Sidel.

Our focus is mainly on the "End of Line" packaging machines, which we have introduced under the Pack-Line series.

We also offer customized packaging solutions to our customers to improve productivity and reduce wastage.

We boast of a highly skilled technical team equiped with advanceddesign softwares, good production and assembly facilities, a well defined and established QC & QA system to deliver hign quality machines backed with excellent after sales support.


 Why
 Us?

Mas Systech will help you, succeed by offering a complete end of the line automation solution in Pack Line series. Our goal is to maximize efficiency in the quality of the product with class technology and class engineering.

Our systems are made in India with guarantee durable and top quality equipment. We boast a to a high quality team in various departments which include, design, production Q.A, sales & marketing.

We having successfully installed many projects for the secondary packaging lines like, Brewery, distillery, glass bottle industry and food industry. We offer the most affordable price in the market over and above our strength is our after sales service, installation commissioning and technical training.
